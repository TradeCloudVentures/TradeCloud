About us

We're the bridge brands and distributors never had.

Every traditional supply chain carries the same hidden cost: the more hands a product passes through before reaching a distributor, the less margin is left for everyone at the end of that chain. Wholesalers, regional agents, and sub-distributors all take their share before a distributor ever sees a price list.

TradeCloud removes those extra stops. We connect distributors directly with the brands they want to stock — no intermediaries standing between a fair price and the people actually moving the product. That direct relationship means better margins, faster communication, and pricing that reflects real demand instead of a chain of markups.

But a direct relationship is only half the job. Distributors also need to know what's moving, what's running low, and how a brand's catalogue performs over time — so we built the tech to back it up. Our platform gives distributors live inventory and sales tracking, so stock decisions are based on real numbers, not guesswork. And when it's time to restock, orders are placed straight from the app — no phone calls, no paperwork, no waiting on someone else to process a request.

The result is a leaner, more transparent way for brands and distributors to do business together — and more of the margin staying exactly where it's earned.
