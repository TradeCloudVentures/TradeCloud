About TradeCloud

The supply chain is broken. We fixed it.

Traditional distribution adds layer after layer of markup — brokers, wholesalers, regional reps — until your margin is a ghost. TradeCloud eliminates every unnecessary step, connecting the brand directly with the distributor and letting both sides win.

We're building for the brands that are done with opaque pricing, slow reordering, and zero visibility into where their product actually goes. With TradeCloud, you see everything — in real time.

F 01

Live Inventory

Every SKU, every warehouse, every distributor — updated in real time. No more spreadsheet reconciliation.

F 02

Direct Ordering

Distributors reorder inside the app. Brands approve, fulfill, and ship — no phone tag, no lost emails.

F 03

Sales Tracking

See exactly what's selling, where, and at what velocity. Make sourcing decisions on data, not guesswork.

F 04

Margin Protection

Agreed pricing is locked in the platform. Both sides see the same numbers. No surprise deductions.
